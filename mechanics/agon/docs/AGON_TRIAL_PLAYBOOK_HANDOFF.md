# Agon Trial Playbook Handoff

## Purpose

Wave VI asks `aoa-playbooks` to own **trial choreography** for the first mechanical Agon rehearsals.

This handoff exists because lawful moves already have center-owned names, but the system now needs recurring scenario routes that can coordinate actors, assistants, expected evidence, owner review, and safe stops before any live arena exists.

## What the center owns

`Agents-of-Abyss` owns:

- the Agon imposition posture;
- lawful move vocabulary;
- owner handoff boundaries;
- stop-lines that prevent protocol drift;
- future arena protocol law.

## What `aoa-playbooks` may own

`aoa-playbooks` may own:

- mechanical trial scenario routes;
- trigger and prerequisite choreography;
- participating role patterns;
- assistant service support boundaries;
- fallback and rollback posture;
- expected evidence posture;
- expected artifacts for owner review.

## What remains forbidden

- no_arena_session_creation
- no_sealed_commit_runtime
- no_live_move_execution
- no_verdict_authority
- no_scar_write
- no_retention_schedule
- no_rank_mutation
- no_tos_promotion
- no_hidden_assistant_contestant
- no_runtime_substrate_claim

## Legal phrase

```text
Trial playbooks rehearse the arena. They do not open it.
```
