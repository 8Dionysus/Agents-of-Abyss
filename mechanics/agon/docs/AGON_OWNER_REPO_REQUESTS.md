# Agon Owner Repo Requests

## Purpose

Wave IV creates owner requests. It does not complete owner landings.

A request is a reviewable signal from the center to a neighboring owner repository:

```text
this lawful move will eventually need your kind of truth
```

The receiving repository may later accept, reshape, defer, or reject the request under its own boundaries.

## Request types

`aoa-techniques` receives practice candidates.

`aoa-skills` receives bounded workflow candidates.

`aoa-evals` receives proof and breach-check candidates.

`aoa-routing` receives gate and dispatch hints.

`aoa-playbooks` receives trial choreography hooks.

`aoa-memo` receives scar/delta/chronicle intake hints.

`aoa-stats` receives non-sovereign derived summary hints.

`aoa-agents` receives actor-form and seat-eligibility hints.

## Center restraint

The center may request, name, and bind.

The center may not implement owner truth.

This keeps Agon from becoming an empire wearing a protocol mask.

## Owner restraint

Owner repositories may later implement their own surfaces.

They must not redefine the center-owned legal move vocabulary unless a future reviewed center wave changes that vocabulary.

## One-line rule

A request is a bridge rope thrown across the abyss. It is not a conquest.
