# Retention Owner Handoffs

Agents-of-Abyss owns the retention and rank law. It does not own durable scar storage, proof legitimacy, derived metrics, actor profile truth, or SDK execution.

Handoffs:

- `aoa-memo` prepares retention and scar-memory candidate intakes.
- `aoa-agents` prepares rank jurisdiction surfaces for actor forms.
- `aoa-evals` prepares retention and mutation legitimacy checks.
- `aoa-stats` prepares derived observability.
- `aoa-sdk` prepares typed helper candidates.
