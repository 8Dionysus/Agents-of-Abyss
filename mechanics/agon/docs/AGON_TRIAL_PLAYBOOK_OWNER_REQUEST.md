## Why this belongs in `aoa-playbooks`

Each trial is a recurring scenario composition. Each route spans actors, assistants, lawful moves, future eval checks, future memo candidates, routing hints, and safe stops. That shape is larger than one bounded skill and smaller than a live arena protocol.

## Acceptance boundary

`aoa-playbooks` may accept these as experimental authored bundles and a compact generated trial registry.

It must not claim verdict, scar, retention, rank, runtime, or ToS authority.
