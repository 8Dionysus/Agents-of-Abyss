# Agon Wave V Center Handoff

Wave V lands a center-facing handoff for pre-protocol Agon gate routing.

The center accepts one narrow routing seam:

> routing may emit an Agon gate candidate when ordinary routing, service handling, or routine cognitive handling cannot honestly continue.

The center does not accept any claim that Wave V opens a live arena.

## Required stop-line

A gate hint is not arena activation.

## Expected routing surface

`aoa-routing/generated/agon_gate_routing_registry.min.json`

## Future dependency

Before the center may define a live arena session model, gate routing must remain thin and pre-protocol:

- it must not issue verdicts
- it must not mutate agent rank
- it must not write scars
- it must not schedule retention
- it must not promote anything to `Tree-of-Sophia`
- it must not decide closure

Wave V is a doorbell, not the door.
