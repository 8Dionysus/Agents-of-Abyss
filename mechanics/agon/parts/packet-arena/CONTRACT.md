# Packet Arena Contract

## Owner boundary

`Agents-of-Abyss` owns packet and arena grammar as center law. Live actors,
dispatch, storage, lifecycle, and runtime bodies remain owner-local.

## Allowed outputs

- state packet shape
- arena/session stop-line
- charter or seat boundary
- runtime-owner request

## Stop-lines

- no live arena session
- no assistant contestant
- no actor-seat activation
- no runtime storage or dispatch
- no verdict or rank mutation
