# Verdict Retention Rank Contract

## Owner boundary

`Agents-of-Abyss` may name verdict, scar, retention, rank, and reputation
contours. It does not own proof verdicts, durable memory, live rank mutation, or
score authority.

## Allowed outputs

- verdict draft boundary
- scar request contour
- retention request
- rank or reputation candidate
- stats handoff

## Stop-lines

- no final verdict
- no durable scar write
- no live rank mutation
- no stats-as-proof
- no memory sovereignty
