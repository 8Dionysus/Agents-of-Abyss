# Sophian Threshold Contract

## Owner boundary

`Agents-of-Abyss` may prepare threshold packets and canon-restraint language.
`Tree-of-Sophia` owns authored meaning, canon acceptance, and threshold
decisions.

## Allowed outputs

- ToS threshold packet
- Sophian owner handoff
- canon-restraint stop-line
- support request

## Stop-lines

- no ToS canon write
- no source-authored meaning claim
- no threshold acceptance by AoA
- no proof substitution for ToS judgment
