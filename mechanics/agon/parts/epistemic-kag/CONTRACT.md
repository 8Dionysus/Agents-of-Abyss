# Epistemic KAG Contract

## Owner boundary

`Agents-of-Abyss` may name epistemic pressure and KAG promotion candidates. It
does not own KAG canon, source-authored meaning, proof verdicts, or ToS canon.

## Allowed outputs

- epistemic move extension
- KAG promotion candidate
- evidence boundary
- owner handoff

## Stop-lines

- no KAG canon write
- no source-authored meaning transfer
- no proof verdict
- no ToS canon write
