# Lawful Move Grammar Contract

## Owner boundary

`Agents-of-Abyss` owns the lawful tongue and move registry contour. Stronger
owners decide whether a move becomes practice, route behavior, proof, memory, or
runtime.

## Allowed outputs

- lawful move name or class
- move registry shape
- move-level stop-line
- owner handoff hint

## Stop-lines

- no live protocol packet
- no final verdict
- no durable scar
- no runtime effect
- no ToS promotion
