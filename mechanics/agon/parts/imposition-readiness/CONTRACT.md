# Imposition Readiness Contract

## Owner boundary

`Agents-of-Abyss` may impose Agon as a center review lens and survival audit.
It may not create live arena execution, actor behavior, runtime dispatch, proof
truth, memory mutation, or ToS promotion.

## Allowed outputs

- survival audit result
- pre-Agon stop-line
- owner route recommendation
- request for proof, route, or pruning review

## Stop-lines

- no live arena session
- no assistant contestant or judge authority
- no runtime dispatch
- no proof verdict
- no memory or ToS mutation
