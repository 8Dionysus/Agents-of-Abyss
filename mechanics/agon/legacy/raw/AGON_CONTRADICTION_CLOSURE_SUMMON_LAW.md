# Agon Contradiction / Closure / Summon Law

Wave X hardens three laws before the arena wakes.

1. An open material contradiction blocks closure.
2. Closure is earned jurisdiction, not default completion.
3. Summon is costly visible intent, not a panic button.

These laws are center-owned because they define the legal grammar of future arena conflict. They do not grant runtime authority, verdict authority, scar storage, retention execution or rank mutation.
