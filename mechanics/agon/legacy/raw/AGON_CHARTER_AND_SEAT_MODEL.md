# Agon Charter and Seat Model

Every modeled arena begins with a charter.

A charter names the laws that bind the session, the opening conditions, allowed lawful moves, forbidden actions, budgets, visibility, and owner handoffs.

Seats are not decorative labels. Contestants must be `agonic` actors. Assistants may serve as steward, notary, monitor, librarian, or other service support. Witnesses are required. Judge seats are candidate-only until future verdict law exists. Summoned seats are intent slots only unless a future summon law grants authority.

Assistants do not become contestants through seat assignment. Any assistant-to-agonic transition requires recharter outside this wave.
