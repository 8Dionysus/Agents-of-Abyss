# Agon Gate Routing Stop-Lines

Wave V gives routing a signal, not sovereignty.

## Stop-lines

A routing gate surface must not:

1. create `ArenaSession`
2. perform sealed commit
3. issue verdict
4. write scar or delta history
5. schedule retention
6. mutate rank or trust
7. grant closure
8. grant summon authority
9. promote to `Tree-of-Sophia`
10. author Agon protocol law

## Allowed shape

A routing gate surface may produce:

- `agon_gate_candidate`
- `agon_gate_candidate_missing_context`
- `owner_review_required`
- `quarantine_hint`

These are reviewable navigation hints.
They are not combat events.
