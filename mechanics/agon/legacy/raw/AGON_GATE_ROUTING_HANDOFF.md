# Agon Gate Routing Handoff

This note is the center-side handoff for **Wave V: Agon Gate Routing**.

`aoa-routing` may grow a thin pre-protocol gate surface that recognizes when ordinary routing is insufficient and an Agon gate candidate should be emitted.

This does not move Agon law into routing.

## What routing may own

`aoa-routing` may own:

- pre-protocol gate hints
- trigger recognition surfaces
- next-hop orientation
- missing-context gate hints
- owner-review routing hints
- quarantine hints for boundary breaches

## What routing must not own

`aoa-routing` must not own:

- arena sessions
- sealed commits
- contradiction ledger truth
- verdicts
- scars
- retention schedules
- rank mutation
- summon authority
- closure authority
- Tree-of-Sophia promotion
- Agon law itself

## Center responsibility

`Agents-of-Abyss` keeps the future Agon law surface:

- lawful move registry
- move owner binding matrix
- future arena session model
- future contradiction, closure, and summon law
- future terminal outcome law
- future promotion boundaries

The routing gate may point to the center. It may not replace the center.
