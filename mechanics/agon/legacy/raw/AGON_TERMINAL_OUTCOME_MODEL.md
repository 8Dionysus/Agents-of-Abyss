# Agon Terminal Outcome Model

Terminal outcomes are modeled as slots only.

Wave VIII seeds the future outcome names:

```text
resolved
revised_and_resolved
bifurcated
deferred_with_cost
failed_honorably
invalidated
quarantined
```

These are not verdicts. They are future landing places for verdict law. No actor, eval layer, stats surface, or runtime may treat them as live authority in this wave.
