# Agon Mechanical Trials over Duel Kernel

Wave XIII binds the first mechanical Agon trials to the bounded local duel kernel.

The center owns the trial-suite law: which trial shapes exist, what earlier Agon surfaces they depend on, which candidate outputs are allowed, and which stop-lines remain absolute.

The center does not execute verdicts, write scars, schedule retention, mutate rank, promote to KAG or Tree of Sophia, or turn dry-run traces into live arena sovereignty.
