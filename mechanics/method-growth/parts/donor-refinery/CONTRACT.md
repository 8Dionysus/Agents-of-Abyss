# Donor Refinery Contract

## Center Boundary

The center may name a donor pattern and route it. It does not adopt donor canon
or declare the extracted pattern owner-local truth.

## Allowed Outputs

- Pattern hypothesis.
- Source and provenance hints.
- Rejection note for contamination.
- Owner request or next route.

## Stop-lines

- A donor pattern is not canon.
- A donor reference is not owner acceptance.
- A reusable pattern is not yet a technique, skill, playbook, proof, or memory.
