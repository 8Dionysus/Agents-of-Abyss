# Owner Landing Contract

## Center Boundary

The center may prepare and track request packets. Only the owner repository can
accept or land the object.

## Allowed Outputs

- Request packet.
- Owner route.
- Required owner landing.
- Proof route.
- Stop-line.

## Stop-lines

- A request packet is not owner acceptance.
- Owner acceptance is not proof.
- A center landing log is not final owner truth.
