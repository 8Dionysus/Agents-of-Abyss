# Growth Closeout

Growth Closeout gives Method-growth work a finish route that is useful for the
next owner and light enough for future agents to follow.

## Use When

- A Method-growth change touches active parts, owner requests, lineage, or
  validation.
- The lane is only a waypoint and another owner must continue.
- Provenance was consulted or intentionally not needed.

## Do Not Use When

- The change is purely local typo repair.
- Another mechanic's closeout contract is more specific.
- There is no owner route or remaining risk to report.

## Route Check

Name changed active parts, archival or provenance sources consulted, owner
requests affected, checks run, checks skipped, remaining risk, and next owner
route.

## Active Outputs

- Closeout summary.
- Owner request delta.
- Validation report.
- Remaining risk and next route.

## Next Route

Route unresolved owner truth to the owning repository, proof to `aoa-evals`,
memory to `aoa-memo`, recurring method to `aoa-playbooks`, and derived summary
to `aoa-stats`.
