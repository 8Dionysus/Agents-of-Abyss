# Growth Closeout Contract

## Center Boundary

The center may summarize Method-growth work and route next owners. It does not
convert closeout into proof, memory, owner acceptance, or final object truth.

## Allowed Outputs

- Changed-parts list.
- Provenance note.
- Owner request delta.
- Checks run and skipped.
- Remaining risk.
- Next owner route.

## Stop-lines

- Closeout is not proof.
- Closeout is not memory canon.
- Closeout is not owner acceptance.
