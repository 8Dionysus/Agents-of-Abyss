# Pruning Contract

## Center Boundary

The center may name why a route should stop, reanchor, merge, defer, or drop.
It does not delete owner history or write owner-local retirement truth.

## Allowed Outputs

- Reanchor route.
- Merge target.
- Defer reason.
- Drop reason.
- Retained lesson route.

## Stop-lines

- Pruning is not erasure.
- Dropped in the center is not owner-local deletion.
- A memory lesson is not landing authority.
