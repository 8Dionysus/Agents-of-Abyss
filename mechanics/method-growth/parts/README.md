# Method-growth Parts Index

Functioning Method-growth parts live here. Each part stays route-shaped and
owner-aware.

## Parts

- [Donor Refinery](donor-refinery/README.md): donor intake, reusable pattern
  extraction, contamination stop-lines.
- [Candidate Lineage](candidate-lineage/README.md): `cluster_ref` to
  `candidate_ref` to `seed_ref` to `object_ref` without center minting.
- [Owner Landing](owner-landing/README.md): owner acceptance, landing receipts,
  and final truth handoff.
- [Pruning](pruning/README.md): reanchor, merge, defer, drop, supersede, and
  prune decisions.
- [Proof Route](proof-route/README.md): evidence and verdict routing before
  promotion claims harden.
- [Method Promotion](method-promotion/README.md): repeated scenario route to
  playbook-owned method.
- [Technique Skill Split](technique-skill-split/README.md): reusable practice
  versus bounded execution.
- [Memory Writeback](memory-writeback/README.md): lessons, retention reasons,
  and recall routes.
- [Maturity Ladder](maturity-ladder/README.md): cross-repo maturity language
  without global score.
- [Growth Closeout](growth-closeout/README.md): closeout route for changed
  parts, provenance, requests, checks, risk, and next owner.
