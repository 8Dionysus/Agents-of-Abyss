# Method Promotion Contract

## Center Boundary

The center may identify scenario-level repetition. It does not become the
scenario-method corpus.

## Allowed Outputs

- Playbook request.
- Scenario contour.
- Evidence requirement.
- Fallback posture route.

## Stop-lines

- Repetition alone is not method.
- A playbook request is not an accepted playbook.
- The center must not own operational choreography after promotion.
