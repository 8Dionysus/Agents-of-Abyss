# Proof Route Contract

## Center Boundary

The center may request proof and name claim boundaries. It does not issue proof
verdicts for growth objects.

## Allowed Outputs

- Proof request.
- Evidence list.
- Blind-spot note.
- Regression posture request.

## Stop-lines

- Doctrine is not proof.
- A passing local check is not a public claim.
- Stats summaries are not verdicts.
