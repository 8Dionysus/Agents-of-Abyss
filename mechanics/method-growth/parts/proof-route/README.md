# Proof Route

Proof Route keeps growth claims weaker than evidence until the proof owner lands
the verdict boundary.

## Use When

- A candidate, method, technique, skill, or maturity claim needs evidence.
- A public or release-facing statement depends on growth quality.
- A regression posture or blind spot must be explicit before promotion.

## Do Not Use When

- The task only names a route and makes no quality claim.
- The evidence belongs wholly inside one owner repository.
- The claim is already covered by an owner-local eval bundle.

## Route Check

Name the claim, supporting evidence, blind spots, regression risk, and which
eval surface must own the verdict.

## Active Outputs

- Proof request.
- Claim boundary.
- Evidence hint.
- Regression or blind-spot note.

## Next Route

Route proof to `aoa-evals`. Route public claims through release-support after
proof exists.
