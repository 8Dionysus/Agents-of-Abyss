# Maturity Ladder Contract

## Center Boundary

The center may provide shared maturity language. It does not force owner repos
to rename local statuses or accept public claims without proof.

## Allowed Outputs

- Maturity reading.
- Status crosswalk.
- Evidence gap.
- Public-claim route.

## Stop-lines

- Maturity language is not a score.
- Public maturity claims require proof.
- Owner-local status remains owner-local.
