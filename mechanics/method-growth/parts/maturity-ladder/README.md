# Maturity Ladder

Maturity Ladder gives cross-repo growth claims a shared reading without
becoming a global score.

## Use When

- The center summarizes maturity across repositories.
- A public claim needs evidence-backed language such as seed, proven, promoted,
  canonical, or deprecated.
- Local status vocabularies must be compared without being overwritten.

## Do Not Use When

- A repository is managing purely local status.
- A claim has no evidence or proof route.
- A score or ranking would replace owner judgment.

## Route Check

Name the local status, the shared maturity reading, the evidence that supports
it, and the owner that can confirm or deny the claim.

## Active Outputs

- Shared maturity reading.
- Local-status crosswalk.
- Evidence requirement.
- Public-claim stop-line.

## Next Route

status truth to the owner repository.
