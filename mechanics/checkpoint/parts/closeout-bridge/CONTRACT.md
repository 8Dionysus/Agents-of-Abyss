# Closeout Bridge Contract

## Center Owns

The center owns the rule that checkpoint-led closeout must stay explicit and
reviewed.

## Must Not Claim

- hidden closeout execution
- final harvest verdict
- progression verdict
- quest closure
- stats authority before closeout

## Allowed Outputs

- bridge request
- ordered closeout chain
- owner handoff hint
- downstream refresh route

## Required Before Output

- reviewed artifact
- checkpoint note or handoff ref when available
- explicit owner route for every survivor
