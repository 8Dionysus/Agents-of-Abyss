# Stress Review Contract

## Center Boundary

AoA may name stress-review questions and owner routes. It may not declare an
owner-local incident repaired.

## Allowed Outputs

- Stress boundary.
- Evidence gap.
- Owner handoff.
- Adaptation candidate.

## Stop-lines

- A stress note is not a repair receipt.
- Degraded mode must remain weaker than normal mode.
- Memory may retain the lesson, but memory is not proof.
