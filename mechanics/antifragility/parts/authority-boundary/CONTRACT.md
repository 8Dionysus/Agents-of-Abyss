# Authority Boundary Contract

## Center Boundary

AoA may identify authority drift and name the owner. It must not seize the work
while correcting drift.

## Allowed Outputs

- Drift finding.
- Stop-line.
- Owner-local correction request.
- Public-claim caution.

## Stop-lines

- Stats derive; they do not reign.
- Memo recalls; it does not certify.
- SDK and runtime helpers must not hide policy.
