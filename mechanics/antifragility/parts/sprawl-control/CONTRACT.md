# Sprawl Control Contract

## Center Boundary

AoA may require a surface-pressure answer before adding tracked surfaces. It may
not use one-in-one-out to delete evidence without owner review.

## Allowed Outputs

- Surface-pressure answer.
- Paired move.
- Exemption reason.
- Cleanup request.

## Stop-lines

- One-in-one-out is not a blind quota.
- Deleting irrelevant material does not justify new sprawl.
- Temporary bridges need an exit path.
