# Via Negativa Contract

## Center Boundary

AoA names subtraction posture and pruning order. It does not delete owner-local
truth.

## Allowed Outputs

- Pruning route.
- Owner-local deletion candidate.
- Provenance requirement.
- Cleanup stop-line.

## Stop-lines

- Deletion is last.
- Evidence must not be erased because it feels messy.
- The target is legibility, not emptiness.
