# Repair Proof Contract

## Center Boundary

AoA may request proof and name the claim boundary. It does not issue proof
verdicts.

## Allowed Outputs

- Evidence request.
- Regression route.
- Claim boundary.
- Eval handoff.

## Stop-lines

- A cleanup story is not proof.
- Memory is not proof.
- A public claim must pass release-support and proof gates.
