# Antifragility Parts

The active parts are the default operating surface. They are intentionally
short: open the part that matches the move, then route to the owner that must
carry proof, memory, cleanup, or choreography.

## Part map

- [Stress Review](parts/stress-review/README.md): turn stress into bounded
  evidence, degraded posture, and adaptation route.
- [Via Negativa](parts/via-negativa/README.md): subtract, merge, move,
  suppress, quarantine, deprecate, or delete without erasing provenance.
- [Authority Boundary](parts/authority-boundary/README.md): shrink quiet
  authority inflation before it becomes owner drift.
- [Sprawl Control](parts/sprawl-control/README.md): apply one-in-one-out and
  surface pressure checks.
- [Fragility Registry](parts/fragility-registry/README.md): track fragile
  patterns without turning them into one-score health.
- [Repair Proof](parts/repair-proof/README.md): route repair claims and
  regression evidence to proof owners.
- [Memory Return](parts/memory-return/README.md): retain stress lessons without
  making memory proof.
- [Owner Handoff](parts/owner-handoff/README.md): make antifragility findings
  portable as owner-local request packets.

## Provenance boundary

Do not open historical source material by default. Use
[PROVENANCE](PROVENANCE.md) when a change needs the old wave source trace.

## Active part contract

Every part keeps three working surfaces:

- `README.md`: when to use the part and where to start.
- `CONTRACT.md`: center boundary, allowed outputs, and stop-lines.
- `VALIDATION.md`: validation route, with executable commands centralized in
  `parts/AGENTS.md`.

A part may grow, split, merge, shrink, or retire when that improves its
function and keeps the route cleaner. The move should leave the active path
easier to follow, not merely smaller.
