# Distillation Mechanic

Distillation is a center mechanic package in `Agents-of-Abyss`.
It names how raw, long, noisy, historical, checkpoint, donor, runtime, or
witness-facing material becomes active, reviewable, owner-routable form without
losing provenance or pretending the result is already proof, memory, runtime,
owner acceptance, or canon.

## Mechanic card

Status: `landed`

### Trigger

Use when material must move from raw, legacy, packet, checkpoint, donor, runtime, or witness-facing form into an active surface without importing noise, severing provenance, or claiming owner-local truth.

### Center owns

Center distillation law, vocabulary, stop-lines, provenance route grammar, active extraction discipline, and owner handoff boundaries.

### Stronger owner split

- `aoa-techniques` owns reusable distillation techniques and practice canon.
- `aoa-skills` owns executable distillation workflows.
- `aoa-playbooks` owns recurring checkpoint, session, campaign, or pilot distillation scenarios.
- `aoa-agents` owns runtime `distill` phase posture and public `distillation_pack` artifact contracts.
- `aoa-memo` owns memory writeback candidates and durable memory review.
- `aoa-evals` owns provenance-preservation proof and abstraction-quality verdicts.
- `aoa-sdk` owns typed helpers, control-panel support, and compatible readers.
- `Dionysus` owns seed, donor, and staging preservation.
- `Tree-of-Sophia` owns compost, principle, and canon-facing meaning.
- `abyss-stack` owns runtime storage, execution, and export plumbing.

### Inputs

- Raw source packet, legacy note, long exploratory surface, reviewed checkpoint, donor harvest, runtime distillation pack, witness-facing artifact, or owner-local evidence bundle.
- Enough source route, review state, and stop-line context to decide what can be extracted and what must remain preserved.

### Outputs

- Active extraction route, provenance bridge, receipt index, noise-pruning decision, candidate handoff, validation gate, runtime-pack boundary, compost-boundary note, or owner-request packet.
- No proof, memory canon, runtime activation, owner acceptance, ToS canon, or final technique/skill/playbook truth unless the stronger owner lands it.

### Must not claim

- summarization as distillation
- proof verdict
- memory canon
- runtime activation
- owner acceptance
- ToS canon or compost authority
- raw deletion authority
- generated or derived distillation authority

## Working Law

Distillation is the lawful conversion of material into a lighter active form
while preserving the route back to evidence.

It may extract, preserve, prune, index, and route. It may not prove, promote,
canonize, activate runtime, or accept owner-local truth.

Generated surfaces may reflect distillation cards, queues, or indexes, but they
do not author distillation meaning.
