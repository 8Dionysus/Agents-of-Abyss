# World Grammar Contract

## Center Owns

- the rule for when RPG language improves agent work
- the stop-line against decorative language
- the route from world-shaped language back to owner truth

## Must Not Claim

- that every task is a quest
- that every label is a stat
- that game tone is more important than exactness

## Allowed Outputs

- a world-shaped route note
- a clearer role or quest reading
- a consequence, reputation, or unlock question routed to the owner
