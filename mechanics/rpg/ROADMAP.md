# RPG Roadmap

## Current Contour

RPG is a readable reflection layer for progression and quest-linked work.
It is not an ontology rewrite or runtime ledger.

## Next Work

- Keep RPG terminology tied to actual source surfaces.
- Route quest items through `mechanics/questbook/` and `quests/`.
- Prepare future projection only with explicit runtime-owner gates.

## Out Of Scope

- Hidden role canon.
- Runtime progression state.
- Owner transfer from skills, techniques, playbooks, or agents.
