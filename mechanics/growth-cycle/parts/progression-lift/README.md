# Progression Lift

Progression Lift names evidence-backed movement without reducing growth to a
universal score.

## Use When

- Reviewed evidence shows a role, process, mechanic, or route became more
  capable.
- The improvement should influence future work but is not owner acceptance.
- A progression claim needs proof or stats boundaries.

## Do Not Use When

- The movement is only vibes, encouragement, or private narration.
- A public claim needs an eval-owned verdict first.
- The claim would override owner-local role or implementation truth.

## Route Check

Ask what changed, what evidence supports it, what still fails, and who owns the
stronger progression meaning.

## Active Outputs

- progression hint
- evidence refs
- proof request
- stats handoff
- owner-local progression route
