# Growth Cycle Provenance

Growth Cycle was landed from cross-repo reconnaissance of existing process
signals, not from a single raw package.

## Source Signals

- `mechanics/checkpoint/`: checkpoint law, closeout bridge, owner handoff, and
  runtime export boundaries.
- `mechanics/method-growth/`: donor refinery, candidate lineage, growth
  closeout, owner landing, and method promotion boundaries.
- `mechanics/distillation/`: raw-to-active extraction, provenance bridge, and
  candidate handoff boundaries.
- `mechanics/questbook/parts/harvest-route/`: quest promotion as public
  obligation rather than scratchpad residue.
- `mechanics/antifragility/parts/repair-proof/`: diagnosis and repair proof
  stop-lines.
- `mechanics/release-support/parts/landing-closeout/`: checked landing closeout
  and public claim support posture.
- Sibling skill signals in `aoa-skills`: checkpoint closeout bridge, donor
  harvest, progression lift, route forks, automation opportunity scan,
  self-diagnosis, self-repair, and quest harvest workflows.
- Sibling owner signals in `aoa-sdk`, `aoa-agents`, `aoa-evals`, `aoa-memo`,
  `aoa-playbooks`, `aoa-stats`, `aoa-routing`, `Dionysus`, and `abyss-stack`.

## Distillation Rule

This package keeps only center law, route grammar, and owner split. It does not
copy sibling implementation details or raw histories into active docs.

If a future source bundle appears, preserve it in a legacy or receipt surface
only when the bundle itself needs durable review. Do not add raw source lists to
active part docs.
