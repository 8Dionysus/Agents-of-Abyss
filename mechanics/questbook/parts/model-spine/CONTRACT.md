# Model Spine Contract

## Owner Boundary

AoA center owns the common Questbook model and source-of-truth routing. Source
quest meaning remains in the quest object store and owner repositories.

## Allowed Outputs

- compact model rule
- source-of-truth map
- stop-line shared by Questbook parts

## Stop-lines

- Do not turn the spine into the full doctrine archive.
- Do not make the model override source quest objects.
- Do not rename local TODOs into quests without a durable obligation.
