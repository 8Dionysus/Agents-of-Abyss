# Lane Owner Routes Contract

## Owner Boundary

AoA center owns ready quest to owner-request route maps. Owner repositories own
acceptance, landing, proof, and closure evidence.

## Allowed Outputs

- route registry
- generated Markdown route table
- ready quest to owner-request mapping

## Stop-lines

- Do not edit generated route rows by hand.
- Do not update owner-request status from a route table.
- Do not close a ready quest from a route table alone.
