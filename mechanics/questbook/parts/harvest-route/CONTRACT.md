# Harvest Route Contract

## Owner Boundary

AoA center may notice recurring quest pressure and route it toward the stronger
owner. The stronger owner lands the reusable object.

## Allowed Outputs

- harvest quest
- owner request
- stronger-owner route note

## Stop-lines

- Harvest does not close the source quest.
- Harvest does not land owner-local truth.
- Repetition should route to the strongest owner, not the nearest convenient
  document.
