# Relation Shape Contract

## Owner Boundary

AoA center owns the relation vocabulary and relation read model. Quest lanes
and owner repositories keep lifecycle movement, acceptance, and closure proof.

## Allowed Outputs

- relation vocabulary
- relation metadata in source quest objects
- generated relation read model

## Stop-lines

- `sidequest` is not ownership transfer.
- Relations do not close quests.
- Relations do not prove dependencies or owner acceptance unless the source
  quest evidence says so.
