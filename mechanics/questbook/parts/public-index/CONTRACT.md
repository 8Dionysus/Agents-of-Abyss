# Public Index Contract

## Owner Boundary

AoA center owns the public Questbook index posture. Individual quest meaning
belongs to source quest files and their lanes.

## Allowed Outputs

- public frontier entry
- near-contour index note
- link to source quest object

## Stop-lines

- Do not turn `QUESTBOOK.md` into a roadmap.
- Do not paste private planning notes into the public index.
- Do not close or promote quests from the index alone.
