# Owner Handoffs Contract

## Owner Boundary

AoA center may request stronger-owner landing. The stronger owner accepts,
rejects, lands, proves, or closes the request.

## Allowed Outputs

- owner request packet
- queue entry
- route note from a ready quest

## Stop-lines

- Do not mark an owner request accepted from Questbook alone.
- Do not close a quest from a request packet alone.
- Do not claim sibling-repo behavior landed without owner-local evidence.
