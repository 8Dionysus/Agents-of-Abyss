# Rollback Return Contract

Rollback is part of a transition claim when reversal would affect public
meaning, owner truth, generated mirrors, release state, or runtime posture.

## Required Before Output

- The rollback trigger is explicit.
- The owner of rollback execution is explicit.
- Provenance survives the rollback.

## Stop-lines

- No hidden rollback debt.
- No runtime rollback claim from center docs.
- No deletion of evidence needed for review.
