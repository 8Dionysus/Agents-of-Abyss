# Direction Surface Review

Use this part after a transition to decide whether entry or direction surfaces
must change.

## Use When

- A transition affects current direction, public onboarding, roadmap contour,
  README wording, start-here route, or direction surface map.
- A public claim could become stale because a direction surface still points to
  old posture.

## Do Not Use When

- The change is internal and does not alter current route, direction, claim, or
  owner boundary.

## Route Check

Review README, `ROADMAP.md`, mechanic `DIRECTION.md`, mechanic `PARTS.md`,
`LANDING_LOG.md`, `CHANGELOG.md`, `docs/START_HERE_ROUTE_CONTRACT.md`, and
`docs/DIRECTION_SURFACES.md` only where meaning actually moved.

## Active Outputs

- Route-surface update.
- No-change note when surfaces were checked and left alone.
- Stop-line against stale direction claims.

## Next Route

Use `changelog-roadmap-split` when the destination is unclear and
`public-claim-gate` when the direction update becomes public-facing.
