# Direction Surface Review Contract

After a meaningful transition, review whether direction surfaces moved:

- README
- `ROADMAP.md`
- mechanic `DIRECTION.md`
- mechanic `PARTS.md`
- mechanic `LANDING_LOG.md`
- `CHANGELOG.md`
- `docs/START_HERE_ROUTE_CONTRACT.md`
- `docs/DIRECTION_SURFACES.md`

## Required Before Output

- Change only surfaces whose future-facing meaning moved.
- Keep entry surfaces short and route-driven.

## Stop-lines

- No stale current-direction map.
- No duplicated phase snapshot in entry docs.
- No direction claim without owner boundary.
