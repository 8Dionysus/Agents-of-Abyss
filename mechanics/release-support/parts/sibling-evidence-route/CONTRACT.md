# Sibling Evidence Route Contract

Sibling evidence must be cited, not absorbed.

## Required Before Output

- The owner repo is named.
- The evidence path or receipt is named when present.
- The center wording is weaker than owner-local truth.
- Missing evidence becomes an owner request or stop-line.

## Stop-lines

- No sibling truth imported into AoA center.
- No generated summary as proof.
- No public claim from private or absent evidence.
