# Public Claim Gate Contract

A public claim is supportable only when:

- the source owner is named
- the evidence surface exists
- the claim does not imply sibling acceptance
- proof-dependent wording routes to `aoa-evals`
- generated mirrors are weaker than source docs

## Required Before Output

- Replace promises with bounded current-state wording when evidence is not
  enough.
- Preserve must-not-claim language near risky assertions.

## Stop-lines

- No unverified public claim.
- No hidden capability promise.
- No center-authored sibling truth.
