# Owner Handoff Packet Contract

An owner handoff packet must include:

- request ID
- owner repository
- owner role
- queue status
- priority
- slice
- center sources
- required owner landing
- proof route
- handoff surface hint
- stop-line
- next action

## Required Before Output

- The packet does not mark itself accepted.
- The proof route is explicit when public claims are involved.

## Stop-lines

- No owner acceptance without owner receipt.
- No center route as owner-local implementation.
