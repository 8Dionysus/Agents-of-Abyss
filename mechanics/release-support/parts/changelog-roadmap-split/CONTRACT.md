# Changelog Roadmap Split Contract

Keep these surfaces distinct:

- `CHANGELOG.md`: released repository history.
- `ROADMAP.md`: current direction and future contour.
- `LANDING_LOG.md`: checked mechanic landings.
- `DIRECTION.md`: mechanic purpose and current posture.
- `PARTS.md`: active operating routes.

## Required Before Output

- Do not copy full landing detail into `ROADMAP.md`.
- Do not treat planned work as release history.
- Do not leave a transition without a landing or changelog route when one is
  required.

## Stop-lines

- No roadmap history as changelog.
- No changelog as roadmap.
- No landing log as proof verdict.
