# Landing Closeout Contract

A landing closeout must name:

- changed active parts or surfaces
- provenance sources consulted through `PROVENANCE.md`
- owner requests affected
- checks run
- checks skipped
- remaining risk
- next owner route if this lane was only a waypoint

## Required Before Output

- Raw source material is not listed directly from active parts.
- Landing does not claim sibling acceptance.
- Unverified claims are left as requested, planted, or blocked.

## Stop-lines

- No landing without provenance route.
- No closeout without validation status.
- No active route polluted by raw source tails.
