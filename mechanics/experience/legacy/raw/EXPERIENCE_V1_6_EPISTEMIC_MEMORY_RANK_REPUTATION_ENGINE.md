## Core Law

The v1.6 center contract is a consequence grammar over the v1.5 epistemic
layer:

```text
epistemic delta / trial evidence
  -> memo intake candidate
  -> retention check candidate
  -> repeated retention result candidate
  -> standing / jurisdiction / reputation candidate
  -> owner review gate
  -> owner-local landing or recharter request
```

Its laws are:

- No standing from a single blaze.
- No reputation event without evidence.
- No rank delta without retention and review.
- No jurisdiction grant without reviewed basis.
- No closure right without contradiction trace.
- No summon right without visible cost.
- No Codex rank or rights authority.
- No assistant agonic rank or jurisdiction.
- No memory or model history as truth.
- No runtime ledger or scheduler authority.
- No direct `Tree-of-Sophia` write and no KAG canon shortcut.

## Standing and Rights Boundary

Standing names reviewed maturity posture. Jurisdiction names specific powers
that may be granted, suspended, restored, or revoked through review. Summon
initiator and closure candidate remain jurisdictions, not decorative rank
labels.

The center contract may only name reviewable candidates after witnessed
recurrence and retention pressure. It may not apply fate. A reputation event is
not a score. A rank delta is not a runtime mutation. A closure right is not a
default consequence of one duel. A summon right is not a panic button.

grant rights, write durable memory, activate ledgers, or promote canon.

## Blocking Contracts

The v1.6 center contract treats archive-local generated rank/reputation clean
flows, dashboards, watch orders, right resolutions, face ledgers, trust
vectors, model-history artifacts, storage records, scripts, tests, and
cross-repo owner proposals as quarantine evidence unless an owner repository
later lands an adapted surface through its own gate.

The center contract requires:

- landing states remain draft, proposed, reviewed, blocked, quarantined, or
  candidate-only; `active` is not a landing state
- live rank mutation, live rights activation, and live runtime activation are
  forbidden
- reputation events require evidence refs, verdict trace, recurrence trace, and
  retention context
- one-event promotion and direct rights grant both block
- retention remains candidate-only and recurrence-backed
- memo may intake candidates but may not become durable truth
- standing and jurisdiction remain owner-local candidates behind reviewed basis
- closure without contradiction trace and summon without visible cost both
  block
- assistants and Codex may not assign rank, grant jurisdiction, or write
  memory
- stats, routing, memo, evals, and dashboards may not become proof or verdict
  authority
- runtime workers, ledgers, schedulers, jobs, ports, and daemons are forbidden
- direct `Tree-of-Sophia` writes and KAG canon claims are forbidden
