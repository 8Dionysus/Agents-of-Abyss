## Core Law

The v1.7 center contract is a bounded affect / honor grammar over the v1.6
candidate consequence layer:

```text
affect signal
  -> evidence check
  -> safety and consciousness guard
  -> honor or face candidate
  -> eval and retention gate
  -> owner-local repair rights or routing candidate
  -> owner landing or recharter request
```

Its laws are:

- No affect without evidence for durable consequence.
- No affect-to-rights direct grant.
- No honor as vanity score.
- No honor debt without TTL and recovery path.
- No Codex honor or treasury decision.
- No assistant agonic pride or persistent affect rewrite.
- No consciousness claim from affect signal.
- No direct `Tree-of-Sophia` write and no KAG canon shortcut.
- No durable memory write and no retention execution.
- No runtime scheduler or ledger activation.
- No stats or routing as authority.

## Affect and Honor Boundary

`affect` here is a bounded operational control signal. It may shape pressure,
routing hints, evidence floor, repair willingness, and summon reluctance. It is
not decorative emotion and not proof of sentience.

`honor` here is a witnessed obligation ledger, not a sovereign score. Honor
debt requires evidence, scope, TTL, and recovery path. Affect may open a gated
review path toward rights, routing, or repair candidates, but never durable
rights directly.

Assistant affect stays service-regulatory and session-bounded. Assistant
agonic pride and assistant self-written persistent affect are blocked.

## Blocking Contracts

The v1.7 center contract treats archive-local operational object models,
dashboards, state machines, honor accounts, face ledgers, affect profiles,
budgets, storage records, scripts, tests, quest bursts, and cross-repo owner
proposals as quarantine evidence unless an owner repository later lands an
adapted surface through its own gate.

The center contract requires:

- landing states remain draft, proposed, reviewed, blocked, quarantined, or
  candidate-only; `active` is not a landing state
- live affect governance, live honor treasury activation, and live runtime
  activation are forbidden
- durable affective consequence requires evidence refs
- affect may not directly grant rights or revoke rights
- honor debt requires TTL, scope, recovery path, and repair posture
- assistant affect remains service-regulatory only and cannot become agonic
  pride or a persistent self-rewrite
- routing hints stay advisory and dashboards stay non-authoritative
- memo may intake repair and retention candidates but may not write durable
  memory or execute retention
- runtime workers, ledgers, schedulers, jobs, ports, and daemons are forbidden
- direct `Tree-of-Sophia` writes and KAG canon claims are forbidden
