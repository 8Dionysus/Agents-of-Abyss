# Experience Certification Law


Certification is the sovereign boundary between advisory experience and persistent service form.

A valid certification must cite:
- the originating experience candidate,
- the bounded verdict that justified change,
- the proposed patch,
- the regression pack,
- the rollback drill,
- the compatibility delta,
- the authorized reviewer decision.

A certification is invalid if:
- it is issued by Codex/session capture alone,
- regression coverage is missing,
- rollback is untested,
- the patch rewrites assistant identity without explicit re-chartering,
- the release skips operator review.
