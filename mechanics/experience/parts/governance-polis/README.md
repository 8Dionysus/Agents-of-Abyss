# Governance Polis

Governance Polis is an active Experience part. It owns polis governance, constitution runtime boundary, council model, votes, vetoes, appeals, stays, quarantine, disputes, precedents, and authority resolver language.

## Use when

Use when Experience needs public decision discipline, dispute handling, governance traceability, or constitutional stop-lines.

## Active outputs

- decision table, policy registry route, appeal or stay route, precedent index hint, and constitution-runtime stop-line.
- Owner handoff language when the center cannot accept or prove the operational slice.
- Stop-lines that keep Experience center law from becoming live runtime, memory, proof, or owner-local truth.
