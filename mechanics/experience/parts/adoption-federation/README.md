# Adoption Federation

Adoption Federation is an active Experience part. It owns federation harvest, adoption gates, owner consent, compatibility, rollback, retention, event kinds, state machines, and owner request routing.

## Use when

Use when a center Experience contract proposes owner-local landing or federation adoption without owner acceptance yet.

## Active outputs

- owner adoption request, consent boundary, adoption state, rollback route, and retention stop-line.
- Owner handoff language when the center cannot accept or prove the operational slice.
- Stop-lines that keep Experience center law from becoming live runtime, memory, proof, or owner-local truth.
