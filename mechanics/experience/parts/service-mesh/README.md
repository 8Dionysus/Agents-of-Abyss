# Service Mesh

Service Mesh is an active Experience part. It owns service mesh law, service operations, Agon and service release compatibility, service posture, and no-runtime dispatch boundaries.

## Use when

Use when Experience describes service operations or service-to-mechanic coordination before runtime owner landing.

## Active outputs

- service operation drill, service law stop-line, compatibility route, and runtime-owner request.
- Owner handoff language when the center cannot accept or prove the operational slice.
- Stop-lines that keep Experience center law from becoming live runtime, memory, proof, or owner-local truth.
