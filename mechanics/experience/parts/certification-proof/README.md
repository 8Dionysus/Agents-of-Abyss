Certification Proof is an active Experience part. It owns certification discipline, watchtower posture, canary gates, regression checks, drift alarms, smoke gates, and proof handoff language.

## Use when

Use when a claim needs gated review, release confidence, regression protection, watchtower evidence, or certification vocabulary.

- certification gate, watchtower note, canary route, regression hint, and proof owner handoff.
- Owner handoff language when the center cannot accept or prove the operational slice.
