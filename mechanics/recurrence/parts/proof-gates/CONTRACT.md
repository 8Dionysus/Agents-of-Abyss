# Proof Gates Contract

## Center Owns

The center owns the requirement that recurrence claims stay weaker than proof.

## Must Not Claim

- recovery success without evidence
- global recurrence maturity
- broad safety or intelligence score
- missing evidence as a pass

## Allowed Outputs

- proof request
- integrity route
- anti-overclaim note
- downgrade recommendation
- owner-evidence request

## Required Before Output

- one bounded claim
- one evidence route
- one proof owner or explicit proof gap
