# Proof Gates

Proof Gates keep recurrence claims bounded until `aoa-evals` can read anchor
integrity, control-plane integrity, continuity quality, or recovery honesty.

## Use When

- A return, reanchor, continuity, or control-plane claim may become public.
- Missing proof would turn doctrine into a success claim.
- Recovery quality, scope drift, verification honesty, or approval boundary
  adherence must be judged.

## Do Not Use When

- The center is only naming a request or law.
- The route wants one proof surface to imply global recurrence maturity.
- Missing axes are being counted as passes.

## Route Check

Ask what claim is being made. If the claim is stronger than the evidence, route
to `aoa-evals` or downgrade the wording.
