# Continuity Window Contract

## Center Owns

The center owns the stop-line that continuity must remain anchored, bounded,
and reviewable.

## Must Not Claim

- permissionless autonomy
- ambient memory continuity
- hidden revision authority
- proof of self-agency quality

## Allowed Outputs

- continuity route
- revision-window boundary
- reanchor request
- anchor-artifact route
- proof request

## Required Before Output

- explicit continuity thread
- explicit revision window
- explicit reanchor or no-drift finding
- explicit anchor artifact
