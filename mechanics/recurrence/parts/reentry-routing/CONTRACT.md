# Reentry Routing Contract

## Center Owns

The center owns the rule that routing may point to recurrence anchors but must
not author recurrence meaning.

## Must Not Claim

- source truth
- memory truth
- budget policy
- direct runtime resume

## Allowed Outputs

- re-entry hint
- source-owned inspect route
- memo recall pointer
- loop guard route
- owner handoff

## Required Before Output

- one stronger source or owner target
- one bounded route reason
- no router-owned authority target
