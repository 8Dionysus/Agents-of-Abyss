# Campaign Route Contract

## Owner Boundary

This part owns center-side campaign shape for audit work. It does not own the repeated playbook after the route becomes stable.

## Allowed Outputs

- scope statement
- surface set
- owner split
- finding index
- closeout route

## Stop-lines

- Do not turn a campaign into a hidden task warehouse.
- Do not keep stale campaign obligations in active docs.
- Do not bypass owner-local closure.

## Validation

Use the validation lane in [mechanics/audit/AGENTS.md](../../AGENTS.md#validation).
