# Source Map Validation

## Evidence Expectations

- The audited path is named.
- The nearest owner guidance is checked.
- Generated, legacy, active, or source-owned status is stated.
- The next route is owner-aware.

## Report Shape

Report source path, owner, authority status, missing context, and next route.
