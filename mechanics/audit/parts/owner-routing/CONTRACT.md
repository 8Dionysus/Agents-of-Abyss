# Owner Routing Contract

## Owner Boundary

This part owns center-side route grammar for audit findings. It does not grant owner acceptance.

## Allowed Outputs

- owner route
- request ID suggestion
- center-source list
- proof route
- stop-line

## Stop-lines

- Do not treat request packets as owner acceptance.
- Do not claim owner-local landing from center docs.
- Do not route around proof when proof is the claim.
