# Evidence Ledger Contract

## Owner Boundary

This part owns evidence posture for center audit. It does not turn evidence into proof or memory.

## Allowed Outputs

- evidence list
- missing evidence list
- freshness posture
- confidence limit
- proof or memo route

## Stop-lines

- Do not overstate evidence into verdict.
- Do not hide skipped checks.
- Do not write memory truth from an audit note.

## Validation

Use the validation lane in [mechanics/audit/AGENTS.md](../../AGENTS.md#validation).
