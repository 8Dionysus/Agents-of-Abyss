# Risk Signal Validation

## Evidence Expectations

- The signal names a source surface.
- The risk is stated in owner-route terms.
- The uncertainty reducer is visible.
- The remediation owner is not bypassed.

## Report Shape

Report risk, evidence, uncertainty reducer, owner route, and residual risk.
