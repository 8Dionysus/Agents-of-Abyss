# Risk Signal Contract

## Owner Boundary

This part owns the center signal that a risk is visible. It does not own remediation.

## Allowed Outputs

- risk statement
- impact surface
- uncertainty reducer
- owner route
- stop-line

## Stop-lines

- Do not make a risk signal a proof verdict.
- Do not make cleanup pressure a deletion approval.
- Do not route around the owner that owns the surface.

Use the validation lane in [mechanics/audit/AGENTS.md](../../AGENTS.md#validation).
