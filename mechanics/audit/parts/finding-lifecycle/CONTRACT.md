# Finding Lifecycle Contract

## Owner Boundary

This part owns state movement for center-visible audit findings. Owner-local execution and closure remain with the owner.

## Allowed Outputs

- finding state
- owner request
- supersession route
- closure reason
- residual risk

## Stop-lines

- Do not mark owner work accepted without owner evidence.
- Do not close a finding by hiding residual risk.
- Do not duplicate roadmap, quest, or release obligations.

Use the validation lane in [mechanics/audit/AGENTS.md](../../AGENTS.md#validation).
