# AOA-Q-AGON-0012 — Technique and Skill Bridge Prep

## Intent

Prepare a reviewed bridge from lawful move vocabulary to future reusable practices and bounded skills.

## Rule

A move may become bridge-ready only when it can be expressed without smuggling session packets, verdict logic, or memory writes into `aoa-techniques` or `aoa-skills`.

## Candidate first families

- `trace`
- `contradiction`
- `closure`
- `revision`
- `evidence`

## Acceptance

Produce a small owner-reviewed candidate list for later seeds in `aoa-techniques` and `aoa-skills`.
