# Code of Conduct

This file is the public collaboration standard for `Agents-of-Abyss`.

## Scope

This standard applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews,
and other public project spaces where people or agents contribute to this
repository.

Security-sensitive findings, secrets, private operational details, and unsafe
logs belong on the private reporting route named in `SECURITY.md`.

## Our standard

We want this repository to be a public place where people and agents can
collaborate clearly, respectfully, productively, and in ways future contributors
can audit.

Expected behavior:
- be precise and good-faith
- criticize claims, diffs, and decisions, not people
- preserve owner boundaries and source attribution
- keep discussion useful for future readers
- sanitize public examples, logs, traces, and config snippets
- route sensitive material through `SECURITY.md`

Unacceptable behavior:
- harassment
- threats
- personal attacks
- repeated bad-faith argument
- deliberate sabotage of discussion or review
- posting secrets, credentials, infrastructure-sensitive details, or private
  operational material in public

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, reject, or route content that harms public safety,
review clarity, or collaboration quality.

Security-sensitive material follows `SECURITY.md`.
