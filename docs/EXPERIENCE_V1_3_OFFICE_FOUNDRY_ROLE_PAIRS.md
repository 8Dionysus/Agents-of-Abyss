## Core Law

The office foundry separates office from kind:

```text
office names the work;
kind names the mode of becoming;
assistant becomes by governed release;
agonic becomes by owner-local trial law;
no artifact may launder one path through the other.
```

One office may have two lawful embodiments. One actor may not secretly wear both
runtime kinds.

## Blocking Contracts

The v1.3 center contract treats archive-local `active` states, scar entries,
approval enums, and generated fixture results as quarantine evidence unless the
owner repository later lands them through its own gate. The center contract
requires:

- same actor IDs cannot appear as both assistant and agonic embodiments
- every pair seed must carry a kind and keep assistant IDs distinct from agonic IDs
- seed landing states remain draft, proposed, reviewed, blocked, quarantined, or
  candidate-only; `active` is not a landing state
- recharter requests may come only from operator, council, or repo owner review
- scar, rank, honor, and retention surfaces are candidate/request-only here and
  durable writes are forbidden
- `Tree-of-Sophia` receives no runtime writes, direct node paths, or canon writes
- routing stays advisory and cannot define office meaning or emit live agonic
  activation
- evals may record bounded verdicts but cannot grant scars, rank, or closure
- fixture/generated-result disagreement is a validation failure, and missing
  pair handoff contracts must block

## Stop-Lines

This landing must not:

- copy raw archive proposals into owner truth
- create a new `aoa-experience` repository
- activate live services, host ports, scheduler loops, runtime workers, pair
  runtimes, storage jobs, or deployment daemons
- approve, certify, seal, release, or pass an office pair by Codex
- let an assistant self-recharter, self-release, self-enroll, self-certify, or
  become an agonic contestant
- create a hybrid runtime mask or cross-kind memory leak
- let an assistant write scars, mutate rank, mutate trust, mutate honor, or
  execute retention
- treat service-to-Agon pair escalation as live summon authority
- grant live arena, live verdict, scar, rank, retention, or honor authority
- write directly to `Tree-of-Sophia`
- let stats become proof, memo become truth, routing become owner, KAG become canon,
  SDK become authority, evals become certification authority, or runtime become
  doctrine
