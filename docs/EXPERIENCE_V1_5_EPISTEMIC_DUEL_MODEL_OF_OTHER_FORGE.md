## Core Law

The epistemic duel forge is a narrow pressure layer over the mechanical arena
kernel:

```text
mechanical duel boundary
  -> sealed model-of-other prediction
  -> prediction reveal after actual moves
  -> prediction accuracy request
  -> countermodel pressure
  -> hypothesis revision
  -> bifurcation quality gate
  -> epistemic delta receipt request
  -> verdict draft request
  -> scar, retention, rank, and memory boundary
  -> owner landing or recharter request
```

Its laws are:

- No model-of-other without sealed prediction.
- No prediction reveal before commit.
- No prediction accuracy without actual moves.
- No countermodel pressure without fair representation.
- No hypothesis revision without explicit epistemic delta.
- No false consensus over material open contradiction.
- No confidence laundering.
- No caricature model and no mind-reading claim.
- No Codex truth verdict.
- No rank, reputation, memory, or scar mutation.
- No direct `Tree-of-Sophia` write and no KAG canon shortcut.

## Model-of-Other Boundary

A model-of-other is not an inner-state claim. It is a sealed, reviewable
prediction candidate about what the other actor may claim, object to, or reason
from under pressure.

The center contract requires:

- evidence references for the prediction
- an uncertainty floor
- fair representation of the opposing thesis
- explicit predicted reasoning move
- expected objection or resistance
- reveal integrity after the other actor moves
- no post-hoc prediction edits

A caricature, stale model, mind-reading assertion, or hidden inner-state fact
claim blocks the epistemic duel. A model may be wrong and still lawful if it was
fair, sealed, and reviewable before reveal.

source_seed_received

## Blocking Contracts

The v1.5 center contract treats archive-local generated duel sessions,
verdicts, dashboards, prediction reports, scar writes, retention schedules,
rank deltas, memory writebacks, storage records, generated catalogs, quest
files, scripts, and cross-repo owner proposals as quarantine evidence unless an
owner repository later lands an adapted surface through its own gate. The
center contract requires:

- landing states remain draft, proposed, reviewed, blocked, quarantined, or
  candidate-only; `active` is not a landing state
- live duel activation and live runtime activation are both forbidden
- model-of-other prediction must be sealed before reveal
- fair representation, evidence references, and uncertainty floor are required
- caricature models, mind-reading claims, and hidden inner-state fact claims
  block
- stale model-of-other candidates block
- prediction accuracy may be requested only after actual moves are available
- post-hoc prediction edits block
- prediction score is not a verdict
- revision requires explicit epistemic delta deep enough to change future
  behavior as a candidate
- shallow delta and revision without delta block
- false consensus over material contradiction blocks
- confidence laundering blocks
- Codex, assistants, runtime, routing, stats, KAG, memo, SDK, and playbooks
  cannot issue truth verdicts
- evals may prepare bounded legitimacy checks but do not gain live verdict
  authority
- durable memory write, scar write, retention execution, rank mutation,
  reputation mutation, trust mutation, and honor mutation are forbidden
- direct `Tree-of-Sophia` runtime writes, node paths, and canon writes are
  forbidden
- KAG promotion, KAG canonization, and KAG source-truth claims are forbidden
- runtime storage records, workers, scoring jobs, host ports, schedulers, and
  daemons are forbidden
- generated clean-flow examples may inform checks but must not become owner
  truth
