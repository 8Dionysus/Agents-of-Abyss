# Self-Agency Continuity

## Purpose

This note names one bounded long-arc continuity law for AoA after the landed
growth-refinery, owner-followthrough, and campaign-cadence slices.

It does not grant runtime autonomy.
It states how a governed route may preserve duration, reflective revision, and
return without losing provenance, ownership boundaries, or human judgment.

## Core rule

Self-agency continuity is not permissionless self-modification.

It is the ability of a governed route to persist across time while keeping all
of the following explicit:

- the continuity thread being preserved
- the bounded revision window currently active
- the reanchor event required when drift appears
- the last valid anchor artifact for return

The practical chain is:

`continuity_ref -> revision_window_ref -> reanchor_ref -> anchor_artifact_ref`

## What continuity must preserve

A continuity route stays honest only when it preserves:

- inspectable anchors instead of vague prior context
- source-of-truth boundaries between doctrine, role, playbook, memo, stats,
  proof, and runtime layers
- rollback and return posture
- reviewability of the active revision window
- answerability to human judgment

## Boundary split

Keep the owner split explicit:

- `aoa-agents` owns role-facing continuity, checkpoint, recurrence, and return
  posture
- `aoa-playbooks` owns recurring continuity choreography once the route is
  truly scenario-level method
- `aoa-memo` owns bounded relaunch support and writeback through existing
  memory kinds
- `aoa-stats` owns derived continuity summaries only
- `aoa-evals` owns proof about continuity quality, bounded revision, and
  reanchor correctness
- `aoa-sdk` may carry continuity hints only

The center names the law and the stop-lines.
It does not become the owner of the route's operational artifacts.

## Stop-lines

Self-agency continuity fails when:

- continuity has no public anchor artifact
- revision widens beyond the named bounded window
- reanchor targets chat residue instead of a named artifact
- memo replaces owner truth
- stats or playbooks claim authority they do not own
- runtime behavior is narrated as healthy continuity without reviewed anchors

## Method posture

Continuity remains smaller than autonomy mythology.

When duration becomes a recurring multi-layer route across doctrine, role
posture, route composition, bounded memory support, derived refresh, and proof,
that route should become playbook-owned method while still returning to named
anchors and explicit owner boundaries.

## One-line rule

Self-agency continuity means bounded duration with explicit anchors and
reviewable return, not permissionless autonomy.
