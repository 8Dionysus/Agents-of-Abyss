# Agon Court / Memo / Stats Prebinding Handoff

Wave VII prepares three future receiving organs of Agon: court, memory, and observability.

This handoff does not open the arena. It asks owner repositories to prepare reviewable surfaces that can receive future Agon signals after gate routing and trial playbooks have produced recurring pressure.

The center owns the request and stop-lines. `aoa-evals` owns proof prebinding, `aoa-memo` owns candidate memory intake, and `aoa-stats` owns derived observability. None of these owners receives sovereignty over the arena.
