# Experience v1.2-v2.0 Bridge

This bridge is the first center move after the `Dionysus` staged intake for the
external v1.2-v2.0 `aoa-experience-*` seed archives.

It is not `Experience Wave 1`; that name already belongs to
`EXPERIENCE_WAVE1_KERNEL.md`. It is not Agon Wave 0, not a numbered
`Dionysus` wave manifest, and not a new `aoa-experience` repository. It is a
center bridge that explains how the staged archive line may later become
owner-local planting waves without replacing already planted Experience or Agon
law.

## Source Frame

The immediate source frame is the staged-only `Dionysus` intake pack:

- `seed_staging/future/seed_aoa_experience_wave0_v1_2_to_v2_0_intake_pack.md`
- `seed_staging/future/seed_aoa_experience_wave0_v1_2_to_v2_0_intake_pack.map.yaml`

## Bridge Route

The route is:

1. `Dionysus` intake and compost
2. center bridge in `Agents-of-Abyss`
3. service mesh operations from v1.2
4. office foundry role pairs from v1.3
5. mechanical arena kernel from v1.4
6. epistemic duel and model-of-other from v1.5
7. rank, reputation, and rights from v1.6
8. affective economy and honor treasury from v1.7
9. context routing nervous system from v1.8
10. continuity loom from v1.9
11. living workspace continuity runtime from v2.0
12. cross-repo hardening
13. `Dionysus` harvest and closure

The route is ordered because later organs depend on earlier gates. Rank cannot
land before trial and retention evidence. Runtime continuity cannot land before
context routing and continuity loom boundaries. Honor and affect cannot grant
rights by themselves.

## Owner Split

- `Dionysus` owns source lineage, staged intake, compost, route discipline, and
  later harvest trace only.
- `Agents-of-Abyss` owns this bridge, center stop-lines, predecessor law
  mapping, and the order of future owner requests.
- `aoa-agents` owns role posture, assistant/service/agonic actor boundaries,
  no-hybrid law, office embodiment, and later continuity actor posture.
- `aoa-evals` owns proof bundles, bounded verdict checks, trial integrity, and
  regression checks.
- `aoa-playbooks` owns rehearsal choreography, recurring scenario routes, and
  trial/playbook composition.
- `aoa-memo` owns memory readiness, recall, retention gates, lineage hygiene,
  and writeback boundaries.
- `aoa-stats` owns derived observability only.
- `aoa-routing` owns advisory dispatch, salience hints, context route receipts,
  and drift watch signals only.
- `aoa-sdk` owns typed helper contracts and control-plane seams only.
- `aoa-kag` owns derived dossiers and pattern candidates only.
- `aoa-skills` and `aoa-techniques` own reusable workflows and engineering
  practice extracted from owner-accepted patterns.
- `8Dionysus` owns shared-root projection law only where later workspace
  projection surfaces are affected.
- `abyss-stack` owns runtime, deployment, storage, and worker substrate only
  after a separate runtime-owner gate.
- `Tree-of-Sophia` owns source-first canon and interpretive intake only through
  its own review path.

## Stop-Lines

This bridge must not:

- create a new `aoa-experience` repository
- copy raw archive proposals into owner repos
- open a live arena
- grant live summon, live verdict, scar write, rank mutation, or retention
  scheduling authority
- grant Codex certification, release approval, rank, honor, continuity, or
  canon authority
- let an assistant become contestant, judge, closer, summoner, scar writer, or
  `Tree-of-Sophia` promotion actor
- install `.codex/continuity`
- activate live services, host ports, scheduler loops, runtime workers, or
  deployment daemons
- write directly to `Tree-of-Sophia`
- let stats become proof, memo become truth, routing become owner, KAG become canon,
  SDK become authority, or `Dionysus` become runtime
